Welcome to the Forms page

One thing I love about the Angular framework is the amount of features and support it has for forms. For this particular form below, I used the ReactiveFormsModule and FormBuilder to build and group the controls. Each FormControl has at least one validator built in. The Submit button will listen to the status of the form and will be enabled only when the form is valid.

Bekah's Input

I created a wrapper component called bekahs-input for the Angular Material Input component to handle multiple input types (text, email, password, date, tel, ext, time, and url).

bekahsPhoneMask is a custom directive puts the "(000)-000-0000" mask on the phone input, and "000" or "0000" format on the ext input.


State Management

When you hit submit, the information you provided in the form will be saved to the state via NGRX in localStorage. The app will then use the information throughout the application. Hit the "Reset State" button located in the nav menu to clear it.
